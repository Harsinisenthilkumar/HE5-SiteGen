Frequently Asked Questions

How does StudySmart personalize my plan?

StudySmart uses your input for subjects, specific goals per subject, and daily available study hours. It intelligently distributes your time, prioritizing subjects where you've set more ambitious goals or need more focus.

Can I adjust my plan after it's generated?

Currently, you can regenerate a new plan by re-submitting the form with updated information. Future updates will include direct editing of an active plan.

What if I miss a study session?

Our plans are designed to be flexible. While we encourage consistency, if you miss a session, you can always input your updated available hours and goals to generate a revised plan that accommodates your current situation.

Is StudySmart free to use?

Yes, the basic study plan generation tool is completely free! We aim to make effective study planning accessible to everyone.
