About Tea & Treats

Crafting Moments, One Sip at a Time

At Tea & Treats, we believe in the simple joy of a perfect cup of tea paired with a delicious snack. Our journey began with a passion for high-quality ingredients and a desire to bring a moment of peace to your busy day. We meticulously source our teas from the finest estates around the world and bake our snacks fresh daily, ensuring every bite and sip is a delightful experience.

Whether you're starting your day, taking a break, or enjoying an evening wind-down, Tea & Treats is here to elevate your ritual. We are committed to sustainability, exceptional service, and creating a community that cherishes good food and great company.

Frequently Asked Questions

What are your delivery hours? +

We deliver daily from 8:00 AM to 6:00 PM. Orders placed outside these hours will be scheduled for the next delivery window.

Do you offer gluten-free options? +

Yes, we have a selection of delicious gluten-free teas and snacks. Please check our 'Menu' section and filter by dietary preferences.

How can I track my order? +

Once your order is confirmed, you will receive an email with a tracking link. You can also log into your account on our website to view order status.

What payment methods do you accept? +

We accept all major credit cards (Visa, MasterCard, American Express) and PayPal. We are also working to integrate more payment options soon!
