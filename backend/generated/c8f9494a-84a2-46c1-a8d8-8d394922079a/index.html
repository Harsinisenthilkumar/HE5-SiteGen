Frequently Asked Questions

We've gathered answers to common questions to help you feel more informed and confident about your visit to Aura Dental Care.

How often should I visit the dentist for a check-up?

For most individuals, we recommend a dental check-up and cleaning every six months. This allows us to monitor your oral health, address any potential issues early, and keep your smile bright and healthy.

Do you accept dental insurance?

Yes, we accept most major dental insurance plans. Our team is happy to help you understand your benefits and process claims to ensure you maximize your coverage. Please contact our office with your insurance details for verification.

What measures do you take for patient comfort?

Patient comfort is at the core of Aura Dental Care. We offer a relaxing waiting area, gentle injection techniques, comforting amenities like blankets and neck pillows, and sedation options for anxious patients. We also take time to listen to your concerns and explain every step of your treatment.

Is cosmetic dentistry expensive?

The cost of cosmetic dentistry varies widely depending on the type and extent of the procedures. We offer a range of options suitable for different budgets. During your consultation, we'll discuss your goals and provide a transparent estimate, along with flexible financing options.
