Frequently Asked Questions

Answers to the questions teams ask most often

From setup to launch and support, these are the common things customers want to know before building with HE5 SiteGen.

Need more help?

Do I need coding experience?

No. HE5 SiteGen is designed for non-technical users, but developers can still export and customize the source code when needed.

Can I publish my website immediately?

Yes. Once your site is generated and refined, you can publish it quickly with our hosting and domain options.

Is the platform suitable for agencies?

Absolutely. Agencies can manage multiple sites, collaborate with clients, and launch polished work in less time.

What if I need support?

Our team is available to help with onboarding, best practices, and launch strategy. Visit the contact page to get started.
